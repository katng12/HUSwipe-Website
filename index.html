MISSION

Swipe Out Hunger (HUSwipes) is a nonprofit organization at Howard University dedicated to swiping out hunger in Washington, DC.

PROJECTS

Currently, we hope to create a systematic process in which students could donate their Dining Dollars and meal swipes in order to alleviate the hunger of food insecure students at Howard University and the homelessness in Washington D.C. Taking on a healthy-eating initiative, HU Swipes also hopes to incorporate more vegan and vegetarian friendly dishes in the cafeterias. In addition to these goals, we volunteer at soup kitchens, such as So Others Might Eat (SOME), to prepare hot meals for the homeless. In these endeavors, we ask for Howard students to join.
